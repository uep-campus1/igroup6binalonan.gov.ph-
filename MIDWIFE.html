Bachelor of Science in Midwifery

About the Course

The Midwifery program focuses on maternal and newborn care, helping mothers during pregnancy, childbirth, and postnatal care.

It trains students to assist doctors and provide safe, quality healthcare for mothers and infants.

What You Will Learn

• Prenatal Care
• Childbirth Assistance
• Newborn Care
• Maternal Health
• Family Planning

Career Opportunities

• Licensed Midwife
• Hospital Midwife
• Community Health Worker
• Maternal Care Nurse Assistant
• Private Clinic Midwife
