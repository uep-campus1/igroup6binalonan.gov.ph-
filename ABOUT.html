About UEP

The University of Eastern Pangasinan (UEP) is one of the recognized educational institutions in the province of Pangasinan, Philippines. It was established with the goal of providing quality and affordable education to students, especially those from the eastern part of the province.

Since its founding, the university has focused on developing students’ knowledge, skills, and values to prepare them for their future careers. It started as a small institution offering limited programs, and through the years, it has expanded its courses and facilities to meet the growing needs of the community.

UEP offers different academic programs in various fields. The university continues to improve its teaching methods and curriculum to ensure that students receive relevant and practical learning experiences.

Today, the University of Eastern Pangasinan plays an important role in shaping responsible and skilled individuals. It remains committed to its mission of providing accessible education and contributing to the development of the local community and the nation.
