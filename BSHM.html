Bachelor of Science in Hospitality Management (BSHM)

About the Course

The Hospitality Management program focuses on hotel, restaurant, tourism, and customer service industries.

It trains students to provide excellent guest services and manage hospitality businesses such as hotels, resorts, and restaurants.

What You Will Learn

• Hotel & Restaurant Management
• Food and Beverage Services
• Tourism Management
• Customer Service Skills
• Event Planning

Career Opportunities

• Hotel Manager
• Restaurant Supervisor
• Flight Attendant
• Tour Guide
• Event Coordinator
