Bachelor of Education Programs

About the Course

The Education program prepares future teachers to develop knowledge, skills, and values for teaching learners in different levels.

Programs Offered

• Bachelor in Elementary Education (BEEd)
• Bachelor of Secondary Education (BSEd)
• Bachelor of Early Childhood Education (BECEd)

What You Will Learn

• Teaching Strategies
• Classroom Management
• Child Development
• Lesson Planning
• Educational Technology

Career Opportunities

• Elementary Teacher
• High School Teacher
• Preschool Teacher
• Private School Teacher
• Academic Tutor
